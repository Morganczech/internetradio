Touch Radio is a modern and privacy-friendly internet radio player focused on
stability, clean design and smooth background playback.

Features:
• Internet radio playback using Media3
• Favorites and station categories
• Drag-and-drop station ordering
• Import / Export stations (JSON)
• 5-band equalizer
• Sleep timer and widgets
• Multi-language support (CZ / SK / EN / DE)

Privacy:
• No ads
• No tracking
• No analytics
• No location permission required (region detected via system language)
• The app works fully offline except when streaming

Touch Radio is fully open-source.
